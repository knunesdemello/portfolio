Hello!

I’m Karina Mello, a graphic design + UX student at SMC.

To me design has been a lens in which to look through to find beauty, simplicity, and functionality in a world that is often polluted by visual information.

I enjoy the process of digging deeper to find solutions that best fit individual needs and expectations, while still finding ways to add in a little wonder. This informs my approach in design for Brand Identities, Mobile Applications, and User Interface.

When I’m not home designing, you can find me on a hiking trail, or taking a road trip to the nearest National Park with my camera. This is where I recharge my battery.
