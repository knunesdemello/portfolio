Architeta

Typeface + poster design | Spring 2020

Architeta was designed with the beauty and simplicity of modern architecture in mind. It displays separate components that seem to connect to each other like lego pieces, yet its rounded counters add softness to this structured font.Stern looking, but with a playful character. This modular font appreciates larger point sizes and a lot of breathing space.
